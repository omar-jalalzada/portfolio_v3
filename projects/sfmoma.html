The Process

  • Information architicture
    Humanized our efforts by re-framing the problems. By taking a step back we understood the context better and did a brain-dump
  • wireframing prototypes
    Conducted in-person interviews with subject matter experts and users. It hepled us distill and analyze key themes and patterns to drive functionality.
  • design guidelines
    Defined a clear path to product based on the user-story and for each individual story we iterated through lo-fi wireframes.
