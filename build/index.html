  • Brief: Re-imagine key aspects of Macy's & Bloomingdale’s iPhone experiences by utilizing rapid design sprints. Research, find and enhance core functionalites in the context of pre-purchase, in-store and post purchase scenarios.
  • Role: Lead UI design and product strategy while assisting with the UX design and research. Utilizied Illustrator & Photoshop for designing the experiencea and InVision to bring the mocks to life.
  • Collaborators: Pauly Ting — Lead UX Designer
  • 1
    Understand & Relate
    Humanized our efforts by re-framing the problems. By taking a step back we understood the context better and did a brain-dump of m-commerce and real-world retail experiences to get a better undesranding.
  • 2
    Research & Analyze Design
    Conducted in-person interviews with subject matter experts and users. It hepled us distill and analyze key themes and patterns to drive functionality. With all that data we created user-personas and user-story workflows.
  • 3
    Prototype & Iterate
    Defined a clear path to product based on the user-story and for each individual story we iterated through lo-fi wireframes. We built an interactive app prototype to test functionalities and gather feedback on the device.
